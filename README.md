🌾 Kisan Setu Saarthi

Kisan Setu Saarthi is a smart agriculture support platform designed to help farmers with real-time information about crops, weather, market prices, government schemes, loans, and disease management. The platform acts as a digital bridge between farmers and essential agricultural services.

🚀 Features

🌱 Crop Guidance & Best Practices

🌦️ Weather Updates

🦠 Crop Disease Information

📊 Market Price Tracking with Graphs

🏛️ Government Schemes Details

🏦 Loan & Financial Assistance Information

🤖 AI Chatbot Support (Module-wise)

🎤 Voice-enabled Assistance

👥 Farmer Community Interaction
